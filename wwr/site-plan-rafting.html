Overview

Purpose

Our whitewater rafting company will help you in navigating rapids by providing you with the tools and/or guidance you need to proceed down the river safely.

Overall, the goal of establishing an online presence is to create a seamless and enjoyable experience for our customers, from the moment they discover our company to the time they complete their rafting trip with us.

Audience

Adventurers and outdoor enthusiasts who are interested in water rafting activities are our target audience for the water rafting website. These people are often physically fit adults between the ages of 20 years and 50 years who have a love for outdoor adventure activities.

Scenario Questions:

  1. Are you an expert water adventurer seeking for a difficult river trip?
  2. Are you a novice seeking a leisurely rafting adventure appropriate for families or school groups?
  3. Are you trying to find a rafting package that includes food, lodging, and transportation?
